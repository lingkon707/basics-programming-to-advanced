## Two Values:
Booleans represent the fundamental concepts of true and false, or yes and no. 


## Logical Foundation:
The concept stems from Boolean algebra, a branch of mathematics that deals with 
logical statements. 


## Conditional Logic:
In programming, Booleans are used in conditional statements (like if statements) 
and loops to control which parts of a program are executed or repeated.
